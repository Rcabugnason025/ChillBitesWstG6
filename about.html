About ChillBites

Bringing the authentic taste of Filipino comfort food to your doorstep since 2020

Our Story

ChillBites was born from a simple passion: sharing the warmth and comfort of Filipino home cooking with everyone. What started as a small family kitchen in 2020 has grown into a beloved food delivery service that brings authentic Filipino flavors right to your doorstep.

Our founder, Chef Maria Santos, learned the art of Filipino cooking from her grandmother, passing down generations of family recipes that capture the true essence of Filipino cuisine. Every dish we serve is prepared with the same love and care that you would find in a Filipino household.

Today, ChillBites is proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers, delivering not just food, but a taste of home and culture that connects families and friends across the Philippines.

Our Kitchen

A state-of-the-art kitchen facility where tradition meets modern food preparation standards.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines by delivering authentic, high-quality Filipino comfort food that brings joy, comfort, and a sense of home to every customer, while supporting local farmers and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To become the leading Filipino food delivery service that not only satisfies hunger but also preserves cultural heritage, creates meaningful connections, and makes Filipino cuisine accessible and loved by people from all walks of life across the nation.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional Filipino recipes and cooking methods, ensuring every dish reflects genuine Filipino flavors.

Passion

Every meal is prepared with love and dedication, treating each customer like family and each dish like a work of art.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers, suppliers, and local communities we serve.

Sustainability

We source ingredients locally and practice eco-friendly operations to support environmental sustainability.
